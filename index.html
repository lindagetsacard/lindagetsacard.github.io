He forgot about Christmas

And with the princess flying away, he nearly lost hope. Until he realized that he is a huge nerd.

This Christmas, I pay homage to how we met. Instead of giving you something tangible or useful, I present to you the very finest in digital Christmas cards - this website!

When we first met, I felt giddy after every date.

You made me want to explore this entire city with you (in our little canoe), and had me hopping from place to place (like a salmon onto a plate of couscous). I'm having an amazing (bike) ride and I hope our relationship climbs higher and higher (up the fire escape).

We've been seeing each other for some time now and I like what I see.

I'm spontaneous and you are adventurous; we're both looking to get the most out of life, and you're always showing me new experiences. You're silly and crazy in the best of ways, and I can relate to that.

And to top it off - you're smart, funny and really pretty!

Although, I may have taken it easy on you.

So don't fret; I may have forgotten some things, but not you. Merry Christmas!

Here's to a new year, together. Remember - just like Mariah says, you'll always be my baby(girl).

Lucas
